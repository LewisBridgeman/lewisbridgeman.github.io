Lewis Bridgeman

Biography

PhD Candidate in Computer Vision & Graphics

I am a PhD student in computer vision & graphics at CVSSP, University of Surrey. I have a particular interest in 3D reconstruction, free-viewpoint video and mixed reality. My work includes multi-view 3D pose estimation, model-based and model-free reconstruction, and immersive playback in virtual and augmented reality. I am currently undertaking a PhD, supervised by Prof. Adrian Hilton and Dr. Jean-Yves Guillemaut.

In 2014-15 worked as a software developer at Figment Productions, on groundbreaking virtual reality projects including Derren Brown's Ghost Train at Thorpe Park, and Galactica at Alton Towers.

In 2017 I graduated with an MEng in Electronic Engineering with Audio-visual Systems at the University of Surrey, and was awarded The DTI MEng Prize for best all-round performance by a student graduating with the degree of Master of Engineering.
